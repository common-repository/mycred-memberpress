== Description ==
Take your MemberPress [membership subscription](https://mycred.me/pricing) process to the next level with myCred MemberPress add-on - **The best WordPress gamification add-on for MemberPress**.

myCred MemberPress gives you the power to award myCred points to your users whenever they subscribe to a membership on MemberPress.

= What is MemberPress? =

MemberPress is an “all-in-one” membership plugin for WordPress that helps you build astounding WordPress membership sites, accept credit cards securely, sell online courses, control who sees your content, and sell digital downloads... all without the difficult setup.

MemberPress will help you confidently create, manage and track membership subscriptions and sell digital download products.

= How can myCred MemberPress help my business? =
myCred is an intelligent and adaptive points management system that allows you to build and manage a broad range of digital rewards including points, ranks and badges on your WordPress/WooCommerce powered website.

With myCred MemberPress add-on, you can build brand loyalty by rewarding myCred points to customers just for signing up or renewing their [membership subscription](https://mycred.me/pricing)!

Create and customize different myCred point types and adjust multiple hooks for multiple membership types.

= myCred MemberPress add-on Features =
* **Reward myCred Points** - Awards default and Customized myCred point types
* **Set Multiple Hooks** - Adjusts Multiple hooks for multiple membership types 
* **Hook Configuration** - Sets limits in hooks for points in days, weeks, months, or no limit.

= Active Events =
* **Award points on the purchase of a new membership** - Give users points when they purchase a subscription product. 
* **Award points when an membership is renewed** - Give users points when they renew their subscription product. 
* **Award points when an specific membership is purchased** - Give users points when they purchase a specific subscription product.

= myCred MemberPress add-on Requirements =
* [myCred](https://mycred.me/) 2.0
* [MemberPress](https://memberpress.com/) 1.9.9
* WordPress 5.0+
* PHP 7.0+
